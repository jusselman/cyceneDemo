Behind the Table

A Life Spent in the Kitchen

Cycene brings decades of hands-on culinary experience to every class and private event — having worked every corner of the restaurant world, from the dining room floor to the heat of the line.

A certified sommelier with a passion for the way food and wine speak to one another, she has spent the last three years building her catering business into one of the region's most sought-after names.

Now, she's opening her table to you. Whether you're a curious home cook, a traveling foodie drawn to the breathtaking beauty of the Columbia River Gorge, or a local looking for something unforgettable — Cycene, Kitch Collective's Table is your seat.
